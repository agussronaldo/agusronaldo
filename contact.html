A Short Review in Multimedia Course as a Student

When i was in the fifth semester of my study in college, which is i was in my internship too at one of the biggest company in Batam, I take my responsibility as IT Programmer and IT Network Technician. One day, one of my adviser asked me if i could design a calender picture for company. He would pay me for that. But i think a little bit more, With no experience and Portofolio at Design, How can i make that ?...

However, i tell him i will try to make as good as he want, But you dont have to pay me, Just buy me some starbucks :) After spending two days, i finished the design, and actually i dont think he will satisfied. But actually he was happy for that, and you know what, my design was printed for 500 copies and distributed to all the employees. Wow, what an achivement. Because of that, its motivates me to improve my skills in design especially for advertising, and once i continue my study in sixth semester, i take the course the related to design. The title is "Multimedia". I will tell you how i feel to that course below.

Obviuosly, There are so many skills and knowledge that i have learnt from this course. First, I learn how to make graphic design. The prodcut is to make Catalogue and Poster. Second, is how to make animation with Adobe Flash Player. On this topic, to be honest i am literally dont know the concept of frames. But i try to understand that in a simple way. How fascinating is that, to make animation by ourself. And the next topic is how to make Game with Flash too. The differences between animation and Game is we have to understand the language of Action Script. So if you understand it well, you can make the game as you want. And the last topic, is how to make a good web design. Actually, i do know how the concept of CSS, HTML, And make Responsive Web in todays generation. But this topic helps me more to make a portofolio by design a web so that you can advertise yourself to the people on the internet.

Final speech... haha, i want to thank you to all the people that help me understand especially the lecturer and the friends. I got a lot of skills that maybe someday i can use it for making my own dollar. It helps me a lot, not just to produce a product, but how to make it good at the result. Thank You...
